# Final Thoughts

The crypto market is contending with sticky inflation, rising yields and new tariffs, which are pressuring risk assets.  BTC, ETH and SOL prices have stalled near key psychological levels and could break either way depending on macro data.  Short‑term traders should prioritise capital preservation and avoid chasing moves; mean‑reversion entries near support with clearly defined stops provide favourable risk‑reward.  For NFTs, focus on communities with strong engagement and be prepared for illiquidity.  Always monitor macro events—especially CPI, PPI, energy prices and Fed commentary—as they are likely to dictate near‑term direction.
